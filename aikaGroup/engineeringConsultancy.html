Engineering Consultancy

Aika Group Of Companies offers full service consulting organization catering to Pharmaceutical and Biotech industries worldwide. We provide project consultancy, training and validation services.

We will provide an experienced project manager who will coordinate the project in close collaboration with your project leader to ensure a successful outcome.

We are guided by a team of full time team members who are all highly experienced technocrats in Pharmaceutical and Biotech Manufacturing, Quality Assurance, Maintenance, Project Design and Execution and Quality Audits. They are supported by experienced functional heads of engineering and pharmaceutical services.

Our network partners have experience in the Asian sub-continent as well as in Europe, Africa and South East Asia. These include many critical dosage form facilities viz cytotoxic formulations for oncology OSDs as well as Injectables, Sex Hormones, Sensitizing Products as well as general product facilities which are designed for global regulatory approval including that of the US FDA, MHRA, EMEA, WHO and MCC.

All these facilities emphasize our efforts at thoughtful design, 100% compliance to regulatory requirements and operational convenience along with maintaining good control over facility installation and operational costs.
