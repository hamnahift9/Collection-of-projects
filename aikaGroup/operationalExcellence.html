Operational Excellence

  1. Home
  2. Operational Excellence

At Aika Group Of Companies, we guide our clients to work smarter and grow faster using design and operational excellence approaches that suit their specific needs and situations. These approaches reduce costs as well as improve the customer experience and business performance against rising competition.

We adopt methods like these to identify issues, form hypotheses, design and conduct analyses, synthesize conclusions into recommendations and help in implementing change:

  • Value stream mapping which exposes waste and bottlenecks in the processes
  • The TPM Approach to Manufacturing Excellence
  • Increasing Overall Equipment Effectiveness (OEE)
  • Reducing the Costs of Poor Quality (poor quality costs a typical company 15-20% of annual sales)
  • Building an effective Employee Suggestion System
  • Improving Quality with the Six Sigma Process
